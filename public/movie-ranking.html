Rowan's Favourite Movies

The best 3 movies I've ever seen

LOTR Trilogy

This might be considered cheating because it's a trilogy and not just one movie. However, since childhood I've watched this trilogy annually and I never get bored of it so for that reason, I have to include it on my list of best movies ever.

Saving Private Ryan

Tom Hanks and Matt Damon, what's not to love? I maintain that this is one of the best war movies ever made as it truly shows the horrors of WWII. The opening scene alone shows the devastation of the D-Day landings.

The Shawshank Redemption

This is still ranked as the best movie ever by IMDB and I agree that it's fantastic. It's a movie that has a truly original story and keeps you engrossed from start to finish.
